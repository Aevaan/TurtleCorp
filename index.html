About TurtleCorp

Founded in 1984, TurtleCorp has been a pioneer in the live reptile logistics sector, bringing warmth, joy, and companionship to households across the globe. We believe no one should face life’s challenges alone — and who better to provide steadfast, unhurried support than a certified, emotionally compatible pet turtle?

Our patented *ShellStream™ Delivery System* ensures your turtle arrives happy, healthy, and ready to listen to all your problems without judgment. With headquarters in 17 countries, we are proud to be the world leader in ethically-sourced companionship turtles.
